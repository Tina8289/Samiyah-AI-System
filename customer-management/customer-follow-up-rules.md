# Samiyah Customer Follow-up Rules


## Purpose


Define customer communication strategy.


Goal:


Maintain relationships

+

Increase conversion rate

+

Prevent customer loss



# Follow-up Principles


## Principle 1


Every customer must have a next action.


No customer should stay without:


- Next follow-up date
- Follow-up purpose
- Responsible action



---

## Principle 2


Follow-up should provide value.


Do not only ask:


"Any update?"


Provide:


- New products
- Market information
- Solutions
- Technical information
- Project support



---

## Principle 3


Follow-up frequency depends on customer stage.



# Follow-up Rules By Customer Stage


## New Lead


Goal:


Start communication.



Actions:


First response:


Within 24 hours



Follow-up:


Day 3

Day 7

Day 14



Content:


- Company introduction
- Product catalog
- Understand customer needs



---

## Contacted Customer


Goal:


Build relationship.



Frequency:


Every 3-7 days



Focus:


Understand:


- Business model
- Market
- Product requirement
- Purchase plan



---

## Qualified Customer


Goal:


Move toward quotation.



Frequency:


Every 3-5 days



Actions:


Provide:


- Product recommendations
- Technical information
- Samples
- Solutions



---

## Quotation Sent


Goal:


Get feedback.



First follow-up:


Within 3 days



Second follow-up:


Within 7 days



Focus:


Understand:


- Price concern
- Product selection
- Competitor situation
- Decision timeline



---

## Negotiation Stage


Goal:


Reach agreement.



Follow-up:


Based on discussion progress.



Focus:


- Solve objections
- Provide alternatives
- Maintain trust



---

## Sample Stage


Goal:


Convert sample into order.



Actions:


Track:


- Sample delivery
- Testing result
- Customer feedback
- Order possibility



---

## Order Customer


Goal:


Increase satisfaction.



Actions:


Maintain communication:


- Production updates
- Quality updates
- Shipment updates



---

## Repeat Customer


Goal:


Develop long-term cooperation.



Actions:


Regular communication:


- New products
- Market trends
- Exclusive opportunities
- Business expansion



---

# Customer Dormancy Rules


## Dormant Customer Definition


No communication:


30 days:

Low activity


60 days:

Dormant


90+ days:

Lost risk



---

# Dormant Customer Activation


Actions:


Send:


- New product updates
- Industry trends
- Successful project cases
- Market opportunities



Avoid:


Repeated price messages.
