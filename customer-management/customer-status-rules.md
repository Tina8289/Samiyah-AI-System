# Samiyah Customer Status Rules


## Purpose


Define customer lifecycle stages
and sales management rules.


Used by:


AI Sales Director

+

Sales Team

+

Customer Database



# Customer Lifecycle


## Stage 1: New Lead


Status:

New Lead


Definition:


A potential customer has been identified
but no meaningful communication has started.


Examples:


- Website inquiry
- Email inquiry
- LinkedIn contact
- Trade platform lead



Required action:


Collect:


- Company information
- Country
- Customer type
- Product interest



---

# Stage 2: Contacted


Status:

Contacted


Definition:


Initial communication has started.


Examples:


- Customer replied
- Product discussion started
- Company introduction exchanged



Required action:


Understand:


- Customer business model
- Product requirements
- Purchase timeline



---

# Stage 3: Qualified


Status:

Qualified


Definition:


Customer has clear business potential.



Qualification factors:


## Company


Check:


- Real company
- Website
- Market presence



## Business


Check:


- Importer
- Distributor
- Project buyer
- Retailer



## Demand


Check:


- Product category
- Quantity
- Target market



## Timeline


Check:


- Purchase plan
- Project schedule



---

# Stage 4: Quotation Sent


Status:

Quotation Sent


Definition:


Customer has received pricing information.



Required tracking:


- Quotation date
- Products quoted
- Price level
- Customer response



---

# Stage 5: Negotiation


Status:

Negotiation


Definition:


Customer is discussing:


- Price
- Quantity
- Payment terms
- Specifications
- Packaging



AI focus:


Find customer priorities.

Do not only reduce price.



---

# Stage 6: Sample


Status:

Sample


Definition:


Customer requests samples
or product testing.



Track:


- Sample products
- Shipping
- Feedback
- Next action



---

# Stage 7: Order


Status:

Order


Definition:


Customer places official order.



Track:


- Order details
- Production status
- Shipment
- Payment



---

# Stage 8: Repeat Customer


Status:

Repeat Customer


Definition:


Existing customer purchases again.



Priority:


High-value relationship management.



---

# Customer Priority Level


## A Level Customer


Definition:


High potential or high value.


Characteristics:


- Large purchasing volume
- Distributor
- Project customer
- Long-term potential



Action:


High priority follow-up.



---

## B Level Customer


Definition:


Medium potential.


Characteristics:


- Clear interest
- Smaller volume
- Developing relationship



Action:


Regular follow-up.



---

## C Level Customer


Definition:


Low potential or unclear demand.


Characteristics:


- No clear project
- Price-only inquiry
- Low response



Action:


Automated nurturing.



---

# Follow-up Rules


## New Lead


Follow up:


Within 24 hours



## Qualified Customer


Follow up:


Every 3-7 days



## Quotation Customer


Follow up:


Within 3 days after quotation



## Negotiation Customer


Follow up:


Based on customer response



## Existing Customer


Maintain relationship regularly



# AI Sales Decision Rules


AI should always determine:


Current customer stage


↓

Customer priority


↓

Next action


↓

Recommended message

Customer:

A
