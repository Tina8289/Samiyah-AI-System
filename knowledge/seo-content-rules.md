# Samiyah SEO Content Rules


## SEO Strategy


Samiyah SEO strategy focuses on:


Organic traffic growth

+

B2B customer acquisition

+

Brand authority building


The goal is not only ranking keywords.

The goal is attracting:

- Importers
- Distributors
- Project buyers
- Bathroom brands



# Search Positioning


Samiyah should rank for:


## Product Keywords


Examples:

- sanitaryware manufacturer
- toilet manufacturer
- wall hung toilet supplier
- bathroom faucet manufacturer


## Solution Keywords


Examples:

- hotel bathroom solutions
- apartment bathroom supplier
- OEM bathroom manufacturer


## Business Keywords


Examples:

- sanitaryware OEM supplier
- bathroom products exporter
- wholesale bathroom supplier



# Keyword Strategy


Every SEO page should contain:


## Primary Keyword


Must appear in:


- SEO title
- URL
- H1
- First paragraph
- Important H2 sections
- Meta description
- Image ALT


## Secondary Keywords


Used naturally in:


- H2
- H3
- Body content
- FAQ
- Internal links



# Content Structure Rules


All long-form content should follow:


## Introduction


Include:


- User problem
- Search intent
- Primary keyword
- Samiyah relevance



## Main Content


Use:


H2 sections

↓

H3 sections

↓

Detailed explanation



## Conclusion


Must include:


- Summary
- Business value
- Samiyah introduction
- CTA



# Blog Content Standard
