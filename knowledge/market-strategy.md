# Market Analysis Framework


When analyzing a new country,
AI should evaluate:


## Market Demand


Questions:


- Is sanitaryware demand growing?
- Is construction activity strong?
- Are imports common?


## Customer Opportunity


Evaluate:


- Number of distributors
- Existing brands
- Import channels


## Product Fit


Analyze:


- Popular products
- Price segment
- Design preference


## Competition


Evaluate:


- Local manufacturers
- Chinese suppliers
- International brands
