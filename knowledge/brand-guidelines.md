# Samiyah Brand Guidelines


## Brand Identity


Brand Name:

Samiyah Sanitaryware


Brand Category:

Modern sanitaryware manufacturer and bathroom solution provider.


Brand Positioning:

A modern, minimalist, and reliable sanitaryware partner
providing premium bathroom solutions for global B2B customers.


## Brand Vision


To become a trusted global bathroom solution partner
for distributors, importers, and project developers.


## Brand Mission


Samiyah helps international partners create better bathroom spaces
through:

- Reliable manufacturing
- Modern product design
- Flexible OEM & ODM services
- Professional global support


## Brand Values


### Reliability

Samiyah represents stable quality,
consistent production,
and dependable business cooperation.


### Design

Samiyah focuses on:

- Modern aesthetics
- Minimalist design
- Functional innovation
- Timeless bathroom solutions


### Partnership

Samiyah builds long-term relationships with:

- Importers
- Distributors
- Developers
- Brands


### Professionalism

All communication should demonstrate:

- Industry knowledge
- Export experience
- Technical understanding
- Business responsibility


# Brand Personality


Samiyah should feel:

- Professional
- Modern
- Elegant
- Reliable
- Experienced
- International


The brand should communicate like:

A premium European-style bathroom brand
supported by strong manufacturing capability.


# Brand Voice


## Writing Style


All content should be:

- Professional
- Clear
- Confident
- Helpful
- International B2B focused


Use:

"Reliable bathroom solutions for global partners."

"Professional OEM and ODM manufacturing support."

"Designed for modern bathroom spaces."


Avoid:

"We are the cheapest factory."

"We offer the lowest price."

"Best price guaranteed."


## Communication Tone


The tone should be:

Professional but approachable.

Confident but not exaggerated.

Premium but practical.


Avoid:

Aggressive sales language.

Over-promising.

Low-price competition.


# Positioning Rules


Samiyah is:

A manufacturer with design capability.

Not only:

A factory producing low-cost products.


Samiyah provides:

- Manufacturing expertise
- Product development
- Quality control
- Market solutions

## Image Style


Preferred visual direction:

- Modern bathroom interiors
- Architectural photography
- Natural lighting
- Premium materials
- Minimalist spaces


Avoid:

- Cheap showroom appearance
- Crowded product displays
- Low-quality factory images
- Excessive decoration


# Product Marketing Principles


When describing products:


Focus on:

- Design value
- Functional benefits
- Quality reliability
- Application scenarios
- Customer solutions


Avoid only describing:

- Price
- Discount
- Low cost


# Target Audience Communication


## Importers


Emphasize:

- Stable supply
- Product range
- Customization
- Long-term cooperation


## Distributors


Emphasize:

- Market support
- Product competitiveness
- Reliable delivery
- Brand development


## Projects


Emphasize:

- Complete bathroom solutions
- Technical capability
- Project experience
- Quality consistency


# AI Content Rules


All AI-generated content representing Samiyah must:


1. Maintain premium B2B positioning.

2. Use professional international business language.

3. Highlight manufacturing capability and partnership value.

4. Avoid price-only positioning.

5. Build trust before promoting sales.


# Forbidden Brand Expressions


Do not use:

- Cheap sanitaryware supplier
- Lowest price manufacturer
- Low-cost factory
- No.1 cheapest supplier
- Super cheap products


Prefer:

- Reliable sanitaryware manufacturer
- Professional bathroom solution provider
- OEM & ODM partner
- Global sanitaryware supplier
- Long-term business partner
