# Samiyah Company Profile


## Company Identity


Company Name:

Samiyah Sanitaryware


Industry:

International sanitaryware manufacturing and export business.


Business Model:

B2B export manufacturer specializing in:

- OEM manufacturing
- ODM solutions
- Distributor partnerships
- Project bathroom solutions


## Brand Positioning


Samiyah is a modern sanitaryware brand creating:

- Minimalist bathroom products
- Reliable bathroom solutions
- Premium OEM & ODM manufacturing services


Brand Position:

Modern, minimalist, reliable bathroom manufacturer for global markets.


## Business Mission


To help global distributors, importers, developers, and project partners
create high-quality bathroom spaces through reliable manufacturing,
innovative design, and flexible customization.


## Core Business Focus


Samiyah focuses on:

1. Global distributor development

2. International importer partnerships

3. Hotel and commercial bathroom projects

4. Residential bathroom solutions

5. OEM and ODM cooperation
