↓

Product Model



AI Agents must always classify products using this structure.

# Product Marketing Rules


When AI creates product content:


Always include:


## Product Value

Explain:

- Design
- Function
- User benefits
- Application scenarios


## Customer Perspective

Different customers require different messages.


Importers:

Focus on:

- Product range
- Market competitiveness
- Stable supply


Distributors:

Focus on:

- Sales potential
- Product differentiation
- Long-term cooperation


Projects:

Focus on:

- Complete solutions
- Technical reliability
- Quality consistency



# Product Positioning Rules


Samiyah products should be positioned as:


- Modern bathroom solutions
- Reliable sanitaryware products
- Professional OEM & ODM products
- Global B2B bathroom solutions


Avoid positioning as:


- Cheap products
- Low-price alternatives
- Commodity bathroom products
