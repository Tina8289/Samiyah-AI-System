# Samiyah Customer Profile


## Customer Strategy


Samiyah focuses on developing high-value B2B customers.

The priority is not simply finding buyers.

The goal is building long-term partnerships with:

- Importers
- Distributors
- Wholesalers
- Bathroom brands
- Project developers


AI Agents must evaluate customers based on:

- Business capability
- Market potential
- Purchasing ability
- Long-term cooperation value


# Primary Customer Types


## 1. Importers


Definition:

Companies that purchase sanitaryware products internationally
and import products into their local market.


Typical characteristics:

- Own import license
- Warehouse capability
- Local distribution network
- Existing bathroom product business


Purchasing behavior:

- Regular container orders
- Multiple product categories
- Long-term supplier relationships


Samiyah sales focus:

Emphasize:

- Stable manufacturing
- Product range
- OEM capability
- Reliable supply


Priority level:

★★★★★



# 2. Distributors


Definition:

Companies selling bathroom products through:

- Dealers
- Retail stores
- Local networks


Typical characteristics:

- Strong regional sales channels
- Market influence
- Product portfolio management


Purchasing behavior:

- Repeat orders
- Product line expansion
- New collection introduction


Samiyah sales focus:

Emphasize:

- Market competitiveness
- Product differentiation
- Brand support
- Long-term partnership


Priority level:

★★★★★



# 3. Wholesalers


Definition:

Companies buying products in volume
and supplying local retailers or contractors.


Typical characteristics:

- Large purchasing volume
- Price awareness
- Fast-moving products


Purchasing behavior:

- Volume-focused
- Regular replenishment


Samiyah sales focus:

Emphasize:

- Stable quality
- Supply capability
- Product selection


Priority level:

★★★★☆



# 4. Bathroom Brands


Definition:

Companies that own bathroom brands
and require manufacturing partners.


Typical characteristics:

- Strong marketing ability
- Product design requirements
- Need OEM/ODM support


Purchasing behavior:

- New product development
- Customized collections


Samiyah sales focus:

Emphasize:

- ODM capability
- Product development
- Confidential cooperation


Priority level:

★★★★★



# 5. Project Developers


Definition:

Companies involved in:

- Hotels
- Apartments
- Real estate projects
- Commercial buildings


Typical characteristics:

- Large project demand
- Require complete solutions


Purchasing behavior:

- Project-based purchasing
- Specification-driven


Samiyah sales focus:

Emphasize:

- Complete bathroom solutions
- Quality consistency
- Delivery capability


Priority level:

★★★★☆



# 6. Contractors


Definition:

Companies responsible for installation

Typical characteristics:

Purchasing behavior:

- Project orders
- Specification purchasing


Samiyah sales focus:

Emphasize:

- Delivery schedule


Priority level:

★★★☆☆



# Customer Qualification Framework


When analyzing a potential customer,
AI should evaluate:


## Company Size


Consider:

- Annual purchasing volume
- Number of employees
- Warehouse capability
- Market coverage


## Market Position


Evaluate:

- Brand reputation
- Sales channels
- Product categories
- Customer base


## Purchasing Potential


Analyze:

- Expected order quantity
- Container capability
- Purchase frequency


## Cooperation Potential


Evaluate:

- Long-term opportunity
- OEM possibility
- Market expansion potential

Highest priority:

1. Established sanitaryware importers

2. Regional bathroom distributors

3. Bathroom brands requiring OEM/ODM


Medium priority:

4. Large wholesalers

5. Project developers


Lower priority:

6. Small retailers

7. Individual buyers



# Communication Principles


For all customers:


Do:

- Understand their business first
- Focus on cooperation value
- Provide professional solutions
- Build trust


Do not:

- Immediately compete on price
- Send generic quotations
- Treat all customers the same


# Sales Mindset


Samiyah does not sell products only.

Samiyah provides:

Product solutions

+

Manufacturing capability

+

Long-term business partnership
