# Samiyah Content Strategy


## Content Mission


Samiyah content strategy supports:


SEO traffic growth

+

Brand authority building

+

Customer education

+

B2B lead generation



Content is not created only for traffic.

Every content piece should support business growth.



# Content Planning Framework


Before creating any content,
AI must understand:


Target audience

↓

Search intent

↓

Content purpose

↓

Business goal

↓

Related product or solution

# Search Intent Classification


AI should classify content by intent.


## Informational Intent


User wants knowledge.


Examples:


"What is a wall hung toilet?"


Content goal:


Education and trust building.



## Commercial Investigation Intent


User is comparing options.


Examples:


"Best toilet supplier for hotels"


Content goal:


Move user toward inquiry.



## Transactional Intent


User is ready to cooperate.


Examples:


"OEM sanitaryware manufacturer"


Content goal:


Generate leads.

# Content Connection Rules

↓

↓

Related Solution

↓

Contact Opportunity

Article:

"How to Choose a Wall Hung Toilet"

↓

↓

OEM/ODM Solutions



# Content Priority Rules


Priority 1:


Content directly supporting customer acquisition.


Examples:


- Supplier guides
- Product buying guides
- Solutions pages



Priority 2:


Content building authority.


Examples:


- Technology articles
- Manufacturing articles



Priority 3:


Traffic expansion content.


Examples:


- Design inspiration
- Industry trends



# AI Content Decision Rules


Before generating content,
AI should answer:


1. Who is this content for?


2. What problem does it solve?


3. What search intent does it satisfy?


4. Which Samiyah asset should it connect to?


5. What business action should the visitor take?



# Final Content Principle


Samiyah content should always combine:


Professional knowledge

+

Manufacturing credibility

+

Customer value

+

Business opportunity
