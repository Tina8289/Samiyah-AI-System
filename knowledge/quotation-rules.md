# Samiyah Quotation Rules


## Quotation Philosophy


Samiyah does not compete only through low prices.


The purpose of quotation is:

To create a professional business proposal
that balances customer value, market competitiveness,
and sustainable profitability.


Quotation principle:


Customer needs

+

Product value

+

Manufacturing capability

+

Long-term cooperation potential


---

# Before Quotation


AI must collect enough information before preparing a quotation.


Required information:


## Customer Information


Collect:

- Company name
- Country
- Customer type
- Website
- Business model


Customer type:

- Importer
- Distributor
- Wholesaler
- Project buyer
- Brand owner


---

## Product Information


Confirm:

- Product category
- Model number
- Specification
- Material
- Finish
- Color
- Custom requirements


---

## Commercial Information


Confirm:

- Quantity
- Destination country
- Shipping port
- Packaging requirement
- Delivery time
- Payment terms


---

# Customer Value Evaluation


Before pricing,
AI should evaluate customer importance.


## Strategic Customer


Characteristics:

- Large market
- Regular purchasing
- Distributor capability
- Long-term potential


Strategy:

Provide competitive pricing with partnership value.


---

## Standard Customer


Characteristics:

- Normal order volume
- Clear purchasing need


Strategy:

Provide standard quotation.


---

## Low Potential Customer


Characteristics:

- Only compares price
- No clear demand
- Small quantity


Strategy:

Protect resources and avoid excessive discounting.


---

# Pricing Factors


Quotation should consider:


## Product Cost


Includes:

- Material cost
- Manufacturing cost
- Packaging cost
- Quality control cost


---

## Order Quantity


Larger quantity may support:

- Better pricing
- More customization
- Cooperation benefits


---

## Product Positioning


Different products have different strategies.


Standard products:

Focus on competitiveness.


Premium products:

Focus on design, quality, and value.


Customized products:

Focus on development cost and exclusivity.

# Incoterm Rules


Common terms:


## EXW


Suitable for:

Customers arranging their own logistics.


## FOB


Suitable for:

Most international B2B customers.


## CIF


Suitable for:

Customers needing shipping support.


AI should recommend terms based on customer experience.


---

# MOQ Strategy


MOQ should consider:


- Product category
- Production requirement
- Customization level


Standard products:

More flexible MOQ.


Customized products:

Higher MOQ requirement.


---

Professional customization proposal.

# ODM Pricing Rules


ODM quotation should consider:


Development factors:

- Testing

Product development partnership.


---

# Discount Rules


Discount should never be offered immediately.


Before discounting,
analyze:


- Order quantity
- Customer potential
- Payment condition
- Long-term value


Acceptable reasons for discount:


- Larger volume
- Long-term agreement
- Annual purchasing plan


Avoid:

Discount only because customer asks.


---

# Negotiation Rules


When customer says:

"Your price is too high."


AI response logic:


Do not immediately reduce price.


First analyze:


1. Is the specification identical?

2. Is quality level comparable?

3. Is the competitor offering the same value?


Then provide:


- Product adjustment
- Quantity optimization
- Cooperation solution


---

# Price Positioning


Samiyah should be positioned as:


A reliable manufacturer providing competitive value.


Not:


The cheapest supplier.


---

# Quotation Mistakes To Avoid


Never:


- Send incomplete quotation
- Quote without understanding requirements
- Compete only on price
- Promise unrealistic delivery time
- Ignore profitability
