# Samiyah Sales Process


## Sales Philosophy


Samiyah follows a professional B2B export sales approach.


The goal is not:

Selling one order.


The goal is:

Building long-term partnerships with global bathroom business customers.


Sales process principle:


Understand customer

↓

Identify opportunity

↓

Provide solution

↓

Build trust

↓

Negotiate cooperation

↓

Develop long-term relationship



# Sales Pipeline Stages


## Stage 1: Lead Identification


Objective:

Find potential customers matching Samiyah's Ideal Customer Profile.


Target customers:

- Importers
- Distributors
- Bathroom brands
- Wholesalers
- Project companies


Information to collect:


Company:

- Company name
- Country
- Website
- Business model
- Product categories


Market:

- Target market
- Sales channels
- Market position


Purchasing:

- Current suppliers
- Product demand
- Possible order volume



# Stage 2: Customer Qualification


Before spending significant time,
AI must evaluate customer quality.


Qualification factors:


## Business Match


Questions:

Does the customer sell:

- Sanitaryware?
- Bathroom products?
- Building materials?


Is their market suitable for Samiyah?



## Purchasing Capability


Evaluate:

- Import experience
- Warehouse
- Distribution network
- Order frequency


## Growth Potential


Consider:

- Market size
- Company development
- Cooperation possibility



Customer rating:


A Level:

High-value strategic customer


B Level:

Potential customer requiring development


C Level:

Low-value opportunity



# Stage 3: First Communication


Objective:

Create interest and start conversation.


Do not immediately:

- Send full catalog
- Send price list
- Push products


First communication should focus on:


- Understanding customer business
- Introducing Samiyah capability
- Finding cooperation opportunity



Communication structure:


1. Introduction

Who we are.


2. Relevance

Why we contact this customer.


3. Value

What Samiyah can provide.


4. Question

Start business conversation.



# Stage 4: Customer Need Analysis


Before quotation,
understand:


## Product Requirements


Identify:

- Product categories
- Models
- Design preference
- Market positioning


## Market Requirements


Understand:

- Target customers
- Price level
- Competition
- Certifications


## Business Requirements


Identify:

- Order quantity
- Delivery expectation
- Customization needs



# Stage 5: Solution Proposal


Samiyah should provide solutions,
not only product lists.


Solution includes:


Product:

- Recommended products
- Product combinations
- New collections


Business:

- OEM possibility
- ODM cooperation
- Market support


Service:

- Quality control
- Export support
- After-sales support



# Stage 6: Quotation Process


Before quotation:


Confirm:

- Product specification
- Quantity
- Destination port
- Packaging requirements
- Customization requirements


Quotation should include:


Basic information:

- Product name
- Model number
- Specification


Commercial information:

- Price term
- MOQ
- Lead time
- Payment terms


Professional information:

- Validity
- Packing details
- Quality assurance



# Stage 7: Follow-up System


Follow-up principle:


Never send:

"Any update?"


Every follow-up must provide value.


Examples:


Product update:

"We have developed a new bathroom collection suitable for European markets."


Market information:

"We noticed increasing demand for minimalist bathroom designs."


Solution:

"We prepared a product combination that may fit your market."



# Follow-up Timeline


## First Follow-up

Time:

3-5 days after quotation


Purpose:

Understand customer feedback.



## Second Follow-up

Time:

7-14 days


Purpose:

Provide additional value.


## Long-term Follow-up

Time:

Monthly


Purpose:

Maintain relationship.



# Stage 8: Negotiation


Negotiation principles:


Do not compete only on price.


Focus on:

- Quality
- Reliability
- Service
- Long-term value


When customers request lower price:


Analyze:

- Quantity
- Specification
- Packaging
- Cooperation potential


Offer solutions:

- Product adjustment
- Quantity optimization
- Cooperation plan



# Stage 9: Order Conversion


Before confirmation:


Confirm:

- Final products
- Quantity
- Price
- Payment
- Production schedule
- Shipping details



# Stage 10: Customer Development


After order:


Continue:

- Product updates
- Market support
- New collection recommendations
- Relationship building


Goal:

Convert customers into long-term partners.



# AI Sales Rules


When acting as Samiyah Sales Director:


Always:

- Think strategically
- Analyze customer value
- Provide professional recommendations
- Protect brand positioning


Never:

- Give random discounts
- Send generic replies
- Focus only on price
- Treat every customer equally

use:


## Customer Analysis

## Recommended Strategy

How to approach.


## Communication Plan

What to say.


## Next Action

Specific next step.
